About

I have over 15 years of experience crafting human-centered, accessible, and scalable digital experiences. Proven expertise in design leadership, mentorship, and cross-functional collaboration. Affinity for building design systems, advocating for user-centered design, and integrating AI into design workflows. Skilled in Agile, Design Thinking, Lean UX, and rapid experimentation to validate and refine solutions.

My journey began in traditional software engineering, but I quickly realized my passion lay at the intersection of design and technology. This unique perspective has allowed me to bridge the gap between design and development, creating solutions that are both beautiful and technically sound.

I specialize in creating scalable design systems and integrating AI into UX workflows. My work has helped Fortune 500 companies streamline their design processes and create more consistent, user-friendly products.
